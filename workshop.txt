description=This mod is a remaster of my precedent [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3007583105]The Last of Guns[/url] mod, taking a different approach. Instead of trying to bring The Last of Us gameplay and mechanics into Zomboid, I've simply added [u]and will continue to add[/u] guns and attachments (both found in the world and craftable) that fit 10 Years Later and The Last of Us universe.
